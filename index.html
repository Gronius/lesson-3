Band concert

Take the chance to hear the Jazz Band in an updated live performance.

Jazz Band are cool guys who play wind instruments and will not leave anyone indifferent. Musicians constantly experiment in styles and forms and do not accept any rules. Their music is unique. The collective's repertoire ranges from jazz standards to energetic modern beats. The collective has many works in its own arrangement.
